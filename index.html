Who We Are

Engineering
A Safer Future

Trusted by organisations across the UK to manage risk, develop people, and protect critical infrastructure.

Onyx Safety Engineering is a specialist consultancy providing safety and risk engineering services to organisations operating in complex and safety-critical environments. Founded by experienced safety engineers, the company was established to deliver independent, technically rigorous advice that helps organisations manage risk effectively while meeting demanding regulatory and operational requirements

We support clients across the full system lifecycle—from early concept and design through verification, validation, and operational assurance. Our work focuses on identifying hazards, assessing risk, and developing robust safety management and assurance frameworks aligned with recognised international standards such as IEC 61508, IEC 61511, and IEC 62443.

Onyx has supported projects across sectors including energy, industrial automation, defence, and critical infrastructure. By combining deep technical expertise with practical engineering insight, we help organisations strengthen system safety, resilience, and regulatory compliance.

⚙️
Engineering Rigour
Our work is grounded in disciplined engineering practice and recognised international standards. We apply structured methodologies such as IEC 61508/61511 functional safety frameworks, IEC 62443 cybersecurity principles, and formal risk assessment techniques to ensure every recommendation is technically defendible, auditable, and practical to implement
🛡️
Duty of Care
We place the safety and wellbeing of people, assets, and the environment at the centre of everything we do. Our duty of care extends beyond regulatory compliance — we work proactively with clients to identify hazards, reduce risk, and support a culture where safety and security are integral to everyday operations
📋
Compliance First
Regulatory compliance is the foundation of effective safety and cybersecurity management. Our approach ensures organisations meet their obligations under relevant legislation and standards, including industry-specific safety regulations, cybersecurity frameworks, and operational assurance requirements. We translate complex regulatory expectations into practical, implementable actions for your teams.
🤝
Partnership
We work as an extension of your organisation rather than as an external advisor. Our consultants collaborate closely with engineering, operations, and management teams to deliver solutions that are technically robust and operationally sustainable. Many of our client relationships continue long after initial projects, supporting continuous improvement and long-term resilience

    Cyber Security
    Services

    Comprehensive cyber risk management, compliance, and incident response tailored to operational and industrial environments.

    • 🔍
      Vulnerability Assessment
      We conduct structured vulnerability assessments to identify weaknesses across IT infrastructure, cloud environments, and operational technology systems. Using a combination of automated scanning tools and expert analysis, we prioritise vulnerabilities based on exploitability, operational impact, and threat intelligence, providing clear remediation guidance.
    • 🧱
      Penetration Testing
      Our penetration testing services simulate real-world cyber attacks to evaluate the resilience of systems and networks. Certified ethical hackers apply recognised testing methodologies to identify exploitable weaknesses and demonstrate potential attack paths. Clients receive detailed reports outlining vulnerabilities, evidence of exploitation, and practical remediation steps
    • 📜
      Cyber Essentials / ISO 27001
      We support organisations in achieving recognised cybersecurity certifications through structured gap analysis, policy development, and compliance consultancy. Our services help organisations align with industry standards, establish effective security management systems, and prepare for independent certification audits
    • 🚨
      Incident Response Planning
      Preparation is critical to managing cyber incidents effectively. We help organisations develop comprehensive Incident Response Plans, define escalation procedures, and conduct tabletop exercises that test organisational readiness. Our approach ensures teams understand their roles and can respond quickly to minimise operational disruption.
    • 🎓
      Security Awareness Training
      [Human behaviour remains a key factor in cyber risk. Our training programmes help staff recognise phishing attempts, social engineering attacks, and unsafe digital practices. Through e-learning modules, awareness workshops, and simulated phishing campaigns, organisations can strengthen their security culture and reduce human-driven vulnerabilities.
    • 🏭
      OT / ICS Security
      Industrial control systems require specialised cybersecurity approaches that respect operational safety and availability requirements. We assess industrial networks, control systems, and remote access infrastructure to identify cyber risks while ensuring operational continuity. Our services support compliance with industrial cybersecurity standards and best practices for critical infrastructure protection.
